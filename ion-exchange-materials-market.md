<p><p>Ion exchange materials are substances that are used to remove or exchange ions from solutions. There are various types of ion exchange materials in the market. Synthetic zeolite is a common type that consists of crystalline aluminosilicates. Polybasic acid salt materials involve the exchange of ions with polybasic acid salts. Hydrous oxide materials are composed of metal hydroxides or metal oxide gels. Metal ferrocynide materials contain metal-cyanide complexes. Insoluble ion exchange materials are often non-porous and do not easily dissolve in water or other solvents.</p></p>
