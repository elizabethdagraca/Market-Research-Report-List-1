<p><strong>The Diffraction Dispersive Element Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ruled Dispersive Element</li><li>Holographic Dispersive Element</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diffraction Dispersive Element market consists of two main types - ruled dispersive element and holographic dispersive element. Ruled dispersive elements are created by inscribing an array of closely spaced grooves onto a substrate, allowing the incoming light to diffract and disperse. On the other hand, holographic dispersive elements utilize holographic techniques to record and reconstruct the desired dispersion properties. Both types are used in various applications that require spectral dispersion, such as spectroscopy, laser systems, and telecommunications.</p></p>
