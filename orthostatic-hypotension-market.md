<p><p>Orthostatic hypotension is a medical condition characterized by abnormally low blood pressure upon standing up. When an individual stands, gravity pulls blood to the lower body, resulting in reduced blood flow to the brain and other vital organs. Common symptoms of orthostatic hypotension include lightheadedness, dizziness, fainting, blurred vision, and fatigue. Causes of this condition can range from dehydration, heart problems, nervous system disorders, medication side effects, or aging.</p><p>In terms of market growth analysis, the orthostatic hypotension market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
