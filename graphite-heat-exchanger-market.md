<p><p>Graphite heat exchangers are devices used to transfer thermal energy between two or more fluids, ensuring efficient heat transfer. There are three main types of graphite heat exchangers: shell and tubes, blocks, and plates. Shell and tube heat exchangers consist of a series of tubes enclosed within a shell, allowing for efficient heat transfer. Block heat exchangers have a compact design with graphite blocks stacked together, maximizing surface area for effective heat transfer. Plate heat exchangers consist of a series of thin graphite plates stacked together, providing high heat transfer efficiency in a compact design.</p></p>
