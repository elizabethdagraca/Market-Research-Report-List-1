<p><p>Nitrogen gas generators are used to produce nitrogen gas for various industrial applications. There are two main types: Nitrogen PSA (Pressure Swing Adsorption) generators and Membrane Nitrogen generators. Nitrogen PSA generators work by separating nitrogen from other gases using adsorption technology. Membrane Nitrogen generators utilize a selective permeable membrane to separate nitrogen molecules from the air. Both types offer a cost-effective and efficient solution for generating high-purity nitrogen gas, with the choice depending on specific application requirements.</p></p>
