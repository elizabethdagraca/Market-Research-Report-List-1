<p><strong>The Pneumatic Drives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piston Drive</li><li>Rodless Drive</li><li>Swing Drive</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pneumatic drives are used to convert compressed air power into mechanical motion. Piston drives are the most common type, utilizing a piston inside a cylinder to generate linear motion. Rodless drives eliminate the need for a piston rod by using a magnetically-coupled carriage for linear motion. Swing drives enable rotational motion by employing a vane mechanism. Other types of pneumatic drives include diaphragm drives, screw drives, and rotary drives. Each type of drive offers different advantages and is selected based on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
