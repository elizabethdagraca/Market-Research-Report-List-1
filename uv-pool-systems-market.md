<p><ul><li>Low Pressure System</li><li>Medium Pressure System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UV pool systems are used for disinfecting water in pools and spas. Low pressure systems use low intensity UV lamps to kill bacteria, viruses, and other microorganisms. They are more energy-efficient and cost-effective but have longer treatment times. Medium pressure systems use higher intensity UV lamps for faster treatment times. They provide superior disinfection but are more expensive and less energy-efficient. Both systems contribute to water safety by effectively destroying harmful pathogens, providing clean and clear water for swimmers.</p></p>
