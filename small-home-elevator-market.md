<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The small home elevator market consists of various types of elevators, including hydraulic, traction, machine-room-less, and vacuum market. Hydraulic elevators use a hydraulic system to operate and are often used in low-rise buildings. Traction elevators use a motor and pulley system to move the elevator car and are more suitable for taller buildings. Machine-room-less elevators eliminate the need for a separate machine room, making them more space-efficient. Vacuum elevators rely on air pressure to move the car, making them a unique and energy-efficient option for smaller homes.</p></p>
