<p><p>Explosion proof thermostats are devices designed to maintain and control temperature in hazardous environments. The market offers two main types: bi-metal explosion proof thermostats and electronic explosion proof thermostats. Bi-metal explosion proof thermostats rely on a temperature-sensitive strip that expands and contracts with temperature changes, activating or deactivating the thermostat. On the other hand, electronic explosion proof thermostats use electronic components and sensors to monitor and adjust temperature. Both types ensure safety in explosion-prone areas by preventing sparks or overheating that could lead to an explosion.</p></p>
