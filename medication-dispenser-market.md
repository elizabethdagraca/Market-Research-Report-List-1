<p><p>The medication dispenser market consists of various types. Pharmacy-based Automated Dispensing Systems (ADS) are commonly utilized in retail or hospital pharmacies to streamline prescription dispensing. Ward-based ADS are designed for use within hospital wards, allowing nurses easy access to medication. Automated Unit Dose Dispensing systems are responsible for preparing individual patient doses in hospitals or long-term care facilities. Other markets within this industry may include home-based medication dispensers or specialized dispensers for specific medical conditions. These diverse market types cater to different settings and patient needs, improving medication management and safety.</p></p>
