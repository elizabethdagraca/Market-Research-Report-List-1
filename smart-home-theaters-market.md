HTIB (Home Theater in a Box) refers to all-in-one systems that include a DVD or Blu-ray player, speakers, and an AV receiver integrated into one unit. On the other hand, Component Systems market involves separate components like a standalone AV receiver, speakers, subwoofer, and media source, allowing users to customize their setup based on their preferences and space requirements.
