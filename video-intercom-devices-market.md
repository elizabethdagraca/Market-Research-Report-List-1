<p><ul><li>Analog Type</li><li>IP Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Video intercom devices are used for communication and visual monitoring purposes. The market comprises two major types: Analog Type and IP Type. Analog type video intercom devices transmit signals through traditional analog cables, providing a cost-effective solution. On the other hand, IP type video intercom devices use internet protocol technology to transmit signals over networks, allowing for remote access and advanced functionalities. IP type devices offer enhanced video quality, scalability, and integration capabilities, making them suitable for larger and more complex setups. Ultimately, the choice between analog and IP type depends on budget, scalability needs, and desired features.</p></p>
