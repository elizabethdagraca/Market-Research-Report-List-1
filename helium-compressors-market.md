<p><p>Helium compressors are devices used to compress and store helium gas for various industrial applications. The market for helium compressors is categorized into two types: air-cooled and water-cooled compressors. Air-cooled compressors rely on the surrounding air to dissipate heat generated during compression, making them suitable for smaller-scale applications. In contrast, water-cooled compressors use water as a cooling medium, allowing for more efficient heat dissipation and making them suitable for larger-scale applications. Each type offers specific advantages and limitations based on cooling needs and application requirements.</p></p>
