<p><p>Conductor bar systems are electrical power transmission equipment used primarily in material handling applications, such as overhead cranes and monorail systems. These systems consist of insulated copper conductors housed in a track, which is mounted overhead or alongside the runway. The sliding electrical contacts, known as collectors, allow the transmission of electric power from the source to the moving equipment.</p><p>The current outlook of the conductor bar systems market is positive and expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
