<p><p>Disposable bioreactors are an important tool in biopharmaceutical manufacturing, and they can be classified into various types based on their mode of operation. Wave-induced Motion Single Use Bioreactors (SUBs) utilize wave-induced motion to create mixing and oxygenation in the cell culture. Stirred SUBs, on the other hand, use mechanical agitation to induce mixing and aeration. Apart from these two types, there are also other variants available in the market, each offering specific advantages and capabilities for different bioprocessing needs.</p></p>
