<p><strong>The Emphysema Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrilobular emphysema</li><li>Panlobular emphysema</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Emphysema is a chronic lung disease characterized by the progressive destruction of lung tissue. There are two main types of emphysema: centrilobular emphysema and panlobular emphysema. </p><p>Centrilobular emphysema primarily affects the central or proximal parts of the lung lobules, resulting in the enlargement of the air sacs. It is often associated with smoking and is more common in the upper parts of the lungs.</p><p>Panlobular emphysema, on the other hand, affects the entire respiratory unit, causing the air sacs to be uniformly enlarged throughout the lungs. It is commonly associated with alpha-1 antitrypsin deficiency, a genetic condition.</p><p>The treatment market for emphysema aims to alleviate symptoms, slow the progression of the disease, and improve the patient's quality of life. This includes medication, such as bronchodilators and corticosteroids, pulmonary rehabilitation programs, lifestyle modifications, and supplemental oxygen therapy. In severe cases, lung transplantation may be considered.</p></p>
