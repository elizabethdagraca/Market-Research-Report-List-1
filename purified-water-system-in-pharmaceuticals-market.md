<p><strong>The Purified Water System in Pharmaceuticals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ion Exchange System</li><li>RO System</li><li>Distillation System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The purified water system in the pharmaceuticals market includes three main types: ion exchange system, reverse osmosis (RO) system, and distillation system. The ion exchange system uses resin beds to remove impurities through the exchange of ions. The RO system involves the use of a semi-permeable membrane to separate water molecules from impurities. The distillation system employs the process of boiling water and then condensing the vapor to eliminate contaminants. These systems play a crucial role in ensuring the purity of water used in pharmaceutical production.</p></p>
