<p><p>Microelectromechanical Systems (MEMS) market types include Sensing MEMS, Bio MEMS, Optical MEMS, and Radio Frequency (RF) MEMS. Sensing MEMS involve the production of sensors that measure various physical quantities like pressure, temperature, and motion. Bio MEMS focus on the development of microdevices for medical and biological applications like drug delivery systems and lab-on-a-chip devices. Optical MEMS involves the creation of microdevices that manipulate light for applications such as displays, projectors, and spectroscopy. RF MEMS are used in telecommunications and wireless devices to enhance their functionality and performance.</p></p>
