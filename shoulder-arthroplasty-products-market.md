<p><ul><li>Hemiarthroplasty</li><li>Total Shoulder Arthroplasty</li><li>Reverse Total Shoulder Arthroplasty</li><li>Resurfacing Shoulder Arthroplasty</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Shoulder arthroplasty products market comprises several types, including Hemiarthroplasty, Total Shoulder Arthroplasty, Reverse Total Shoulder Arthroplasty, and Resurfacing Shoulder Arthroplasty. Hemiarthroplasty involves replacing only the humeral head, while Total Shoulder Arthroplasty replaces both the humeral head and the glenoid. Reverse Total Shoulder Arthroplasty reverses the ball-and-socket configuration, beneficial for patients with severe rotator cuff tears. Resurfacing Shoulder Arthroplasty involves capping the humeral head instead of replacing it entirely. Each type offers specific benefits for patients with varying shoulder conditions.</p></p>
