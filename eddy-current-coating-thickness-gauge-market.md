<p><p>Eddy current coating thickness gauge is a non-destructive, handheld device used to measure the thickness of non-magnetic coatings over magnetic substrates. It operates on the principle of electromagnetic induction, where a changing magnetic field induces eddy currents in the metallic substrate, creating a magnetic field with a specific strength. By measuring the strength of this induced magnetic field, the gauge can determine the coating thickness.</p><p>The Eddy Current Coating Thickness Gauge Market is expected to witness substantial growth during the forecast period.
