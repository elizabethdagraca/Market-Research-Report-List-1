<p><ul><li>Reverse Osmosis (RO)</li><li>Multi-Stage Flash (MSF)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The desalination facility market encompasses various types, including Reverse Osmosis (RO), Multi-Stage Flash (MSF), and Others. Reverse Osmosis utilizes a semipermeable membrane to separate salt and impurities from water, producing fresh drinking water. Multi-Stage Flash employs high temperatures to evaporate seawater, condensing the steam into freshwater. The "Others" category comprises alternative technologies such as Electrodialysis (ED) and Nanofiltration (NF), which purify water through ion exchange and smaller pore sizes, respectively. These desalination methods address the global need for fresh water supplies and provide solutions for regions facing water scarcity.</p></p>
