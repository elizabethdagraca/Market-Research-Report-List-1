<p><p>The Automotive Automated Parking System market consists of three main types: Mechanical System, Semi-automatic System, and Automatic System. A Mechanical System utilizes mechanical components like elevators and conveyors to park the vehicles. A Semi-automatic System involves a combination of manual driver input and automated processes. An Automatic System integrates advanced technologies such as sensors, cameras, and algorithms to fully automate the parking process without the need for human assistance. These systems aim to enhance parking efficiency, safety, and convenience for drivers in various automotive applications.</p></p>
