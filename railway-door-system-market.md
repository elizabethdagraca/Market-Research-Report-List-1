<p><p>The railway door system market is comprised of various types of doors, including airtight doors, concealed doors, and sliding plug doors. Airtight doors are designed to seal tightly, ensuring no air leakage between compartments. Concealed doors are integrated seamlessly into the train's exterior, maintaining a sleek appearance. Sliding plug doors slide open and close horizontally, creating a smooth and efficient entry and exit for passengers. These door types cater to different requirements and functionalities within the railway industry, offering diverse solutions for train operators and passengers.</p></p>
