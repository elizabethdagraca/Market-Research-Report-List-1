<p><p>Feed preservatives market consists of various types such as feed acidifiers, mold inhibitors, feed antioxidants, and anticaking agents. Feed acidifiers help in maintaining proper pH levels and promoting digestion in animals. Mold inhibitors prevent the growth of mold and fungi in animal feed, ensuring its quality and safety. Feed antioxidants minimize the oxidation process in feed, extending its shelf life and preserving its nutritional value. Anticaking agents prevent the formation of lumps and maintain the free-flowing nature of feed, ensuring easy handling and efficient feeding of animals.</p></p>
