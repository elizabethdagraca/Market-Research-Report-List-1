<p><p>The string trimmer market offers three main types of trimmer heads: fixed head, automatic feed, and bump feed. Fixed head trimmers have a permanent line attached that needs to be manually replaced once worn out. Automatic feed trimmers have a mechanism that releases more line as needed, ensuring a continuous cutting experience. Lastly, bump feed trimmers require the user to tap the head on the ground to release additional line. These variations cater to different user preferences and provide convenient solutions for maintaining your lawn or garden.</p></p>
