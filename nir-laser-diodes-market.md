<p><p>The NIR Laser Diodes Market includes two main types: Single Mode and Multi Mode. Single Mode laser diodes emit a single mode of light, resulting in a narrow and focused beam. These are suitable for applications requiring precision and high-resolution, such as optical data storage and fiber optic communication. On the other hand, Multi Mode laser diodes emit multiple modes of light, producing a wider beam. They are commonly used in applications where a broader and less focused beam is needed, such as barcode scanning and display systems.</p></p>
