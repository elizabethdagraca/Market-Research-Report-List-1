<p><p>Hybrid contact lenses are a type of vision correction product that combine the features of both rigid gas permeable (RGP) and soft contact lenses. They consist of a rigid center that provides clear optics and stability, surrounded by a soft skirt that enhances comfort. These lenses are designed to provide the best of both worlds - crisp vision and wearing comfort.</p><p>The market for hybrid contact lenses is witnessing steady growth due to several factors. Firstly, the growing prevalence of myopia and astigmatism among the global population is driving the demand for advanced vision correction solutions like hybrid lenses.
