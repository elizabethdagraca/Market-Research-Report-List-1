<p><p>The Data Buoy market consists of two main types: solar powered and battery powered. Solar powered data buoys make use of solar panels to generate electricity, which is then used to power the buoy's sensors and communication systems. These buoys are commonly used in areas with ample sunlight availability. On the other hand, battery powered data buoys rely on rechargeable batteries for their power needs. These buoys are preferred in areas with limited sun exposure or where continuous power supply is required, as they can operate even at night or during low light conditions.</p></p>
