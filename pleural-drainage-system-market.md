<p><p>The Pleural Drainage System market consists of two main types: Transudative Pleural Effusions and Exudative Pleural Effusions. Transudative Pleural Effusions occur when excess fluid builds up in the pleural space due to imbalances in pressure or fluid levels. Exudative Pleural Effusions, on the other hand, are caused by inflammation or infection. Both types require medical intervention and can be treated with a Pleural Drainage System, which helps in draining the excess fluid and relieving symptoms. Market analysis reveals significant demand for such systems to address these conditions.</p></p>
