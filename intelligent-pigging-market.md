<p><p>Intelligent pigging, also known as inline inspection, is a method used in pipelines to assess their integrity. There are different market types for intelligent pigging, including magnetic flux leakage (MFL), ultrasonic, and caliper. MFL utilizes magnetic fields to detect anomalies like corrosion or wall loss. Ultrasonic technology uses sound waves to measure thickness and identify defects within the pipeline. Caliper pigging involves using mechanical calipers to measure the internal diameter and detect deformations. These market types help companies ensure pipeline safety and avoid potential leaks or failures.</p></p>
