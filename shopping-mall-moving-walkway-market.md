<p><p>A shopping mall moving walkway is a type of conveyor system designed to transport people effortlessly. These walkways can be classified into different market types. Parallel walkways comprise a set of parallel moving belts with individual tracks, allowing people to walk side by side. Multi-parallel walkways have multiple sets of parallel belts, accommodating a larger number of people moving in different directions. Other market types may include curved or inclined walkways, providing convenience in areas with limited space or different elevation levels. These varieties of walkways cater to the specific needs and constraints of different shopping mall layouts and foot traffic patterns.</p></p>
