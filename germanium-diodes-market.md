<p><strong>The Germanium Diodes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>GD</li><li>OA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Germanium Diodes Market Types can be classified into two categories: GD (General Purpose Diodes) and OA (Oxide-Alloy Diodes). GD diodes are commonly utilized in various electronic devices due to their low forward voltage and fast switching speed. They find applications in radio circuits, detectors, amplifiers, and voltage regulators. On the other hand, OA diodes are known for their high current handling capacity and excellent performance at high frequencies. They are predominantly used in microwave applications, such as RF amplifiers and mixers, as well as in radar systems.</p></p>
