<p><p>Cardiopulmonary Bypass Accessory Equipment refers to the additional devices used alongside the heart-lung machine during heart surgeries. Single Roller Pump is a type of accessory that utilizes a single roller to create a pulsatile flow of blood during bypass surgeries. On the other hand, Double Roller Pump, as its name suggests, contains two rollers that generate a non-pulsatile flow of blood. Both types are essential in maintaining circulation and oxygenation throughout the body during cardiac procedures, but they differ in the type of blood flow they produce.</p></p>
