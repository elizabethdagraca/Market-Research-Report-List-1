<p><p>The High Volume Low Pressure (HVLP) spray guns market includes two types: electrical airless spray guns and those equipped with air compressors. Electrical airless spray guns utilize an electric motor to create pressure for spraying paint or other coatings with reduced overspray and fine atomization. On the other hand, spray guns with air compressors use compressed air to generate pressure for a more efficient and controlled application of coatings. Both types are commonly used in industries such as automotive, construction, and furniture for achieving high-quality finishes.</p></p>
