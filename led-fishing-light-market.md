<p><p>The LED fishing light market comprises two main types: water lights and underwater lights. Water lights are designed to attract fish by creating a bright source of illumination on the surface of the water. These lights are typically used in shallow fishing areas such as ponds, lakes, and rivers. On the other hand, underwater lights are specifically designed for deep-sea fishing. Mounted below the water surface, they emit a high-intensity beam of light to attract fish from below and improve visibility for anglers.</p></p>
