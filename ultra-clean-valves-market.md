<p><p>The market for Ultra Clean Valves encompasses three types: Surface Mount Valves, In-Line 2 Port Valves, and In-Line 3 Port Valves. Surface Mount Valves are designed to be directly mounted onto a circuit board, facilitating easy integration into electronic devices. In-Line 2 Port Valves are used for controlling fluid flow in a straight through pathway, while In-Line 3 Port Valves provide the additional capability of diverting fluid flow in multiple directions. These valves are all geared towards providing high cleanliness levels, making them suitable for industries with stringent cleanliness requirements such as semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
