<p><strong>The Gene Panel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Amplicon-based Approach</li><li>Hybridization-based Approach</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gene panel market types include the amplicon-based approach and hybridization-based approach. In the amplicon-based approach, specific DNA regions are amplified and sequenced to identify genetic variants. This method is cost-effective and suitable for targeted sequencing. On the other hand, the hybridization-based approach relies on hybridizing DNA fragments to a complementary probe. It allows for the sequencing of multiple genes simultaneously, making it more efficient for large-scale studies. Both approaches aid in analyzing genetic variations and identifying disease-causing mutations, contributing to personalized medicine and genetic research.</p></p>
