<p><p>The Jacquard machine market can be classified into two types: single-sided and double-sided machines. Single-sided Jacquard machines are equipped with pattern cards on one side only, allowing for the production of intricate designs on one surface of the fabric. On the other hand, double-sided Jacquard machines have pattern cards on both sides, enabling the creation of intricate patterns on both sides of the fabric simultaneously. These different market types cater to various customer needs and preferences in terms of design complexity and production efficiency.</p></p>
