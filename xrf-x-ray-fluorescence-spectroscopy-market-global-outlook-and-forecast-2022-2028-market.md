<p><p>X-ray Fluorescence (XRF) Spectroscopy is a non-destructive analytical technique used for the qualitative and quantitative determination of elemental compositions in various materials. These emitted X-rays are then detected and analyzed to identify and quantify the elements present in the sample.</p><p>The XRF Spectroscopy market is anticipated to experience substantial growth during the forecast period of 2022-2028.
