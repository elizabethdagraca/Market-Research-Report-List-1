<p><strong>The Smart CBCT Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Large FOV</li><li>Medium FOV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart CBCT (Cone Beam Computed Tomography) market can be categorized into two types based on the size of the field of view (FOV). Large FOV refers to systems that offer a wider scan area capable of capturing detailed images of the entire craniofacial region, making it suitable for dental implant planning and orthodontic evaluations. On the other hand, Medium FOV systems have a smaller scan area, mainly focused on specific regions like the maxilla or mandible, which is beneficial for endodontic procedures and general diagnostic imaging in dentistry.</p></p>
