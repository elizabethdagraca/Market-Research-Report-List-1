<p><p>The Electrodialysis System and Equipment market includes three types of membranes: Heterogeneous Membrane, Homogeneous Membrane, and Bipolar Membrane. Heterogeneous Membrane is made of multiple layers with different properties, allowing for selective ion transport. Homogeneous Membrane is a single-layered membrane with uniform properties, providing equal ion transport. Bipolar Membrane contains both anion and cation selective layers, allowing for effective separation of ions. These membranes play a crucial role in the electrodialysis process, enabling the separation and purification of ions in various industrial water treatment applications.</p></p>
