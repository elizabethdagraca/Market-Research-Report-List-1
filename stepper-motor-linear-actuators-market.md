<p><strong>The Stepper Motor Linear Actuators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Stepper motor linear actuators are used for converting rotary motion into linear motion. Captive stepper motor linear actuators have an internal lead screw that limits their linear motion range. They are ideal for applications requiring precise positioning. Non-captive stepper motor linear actuators lack an internal lead screw, allowing for continuous linear motion beyond their physical limits. They are commonly used in applications where high speeds and long distances are required. External stepper motor linear actuators consist of a stepper motor and a separate lead screw assembly, allowing for greater flexibility in design and customization.</p></p>
