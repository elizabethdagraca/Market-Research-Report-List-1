<p><p>A Reduced Pressure Zone (RPZ) valve is a type of backflow prevention device used to protect potable water supplies from contamination. It is commonly used in industrial and commercial buildings, as well as in homes with irrigation systems or fire sprinklers. The RPZ valve works by creating a barrier between the potable water supply and the potential source of contamination, such as a sprinkler system or a chemical storage tank. It uses a series of check valves and a relief valve to ensure that water flows in only one direction, preventing the backflow of contaminated water into the potable water supply.</p><p>The RPZ valve market is expected to exhibit substantial growth during the forecast period. The increasing concerns regarding water quality and the need for stringent water safety regulations are driving the demand for backflow prevention devices like RPZ valves.
