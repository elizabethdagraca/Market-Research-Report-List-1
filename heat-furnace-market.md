<p><p>Heat furnace market can be divided into two main types: atmosphere furnaces and vacuum furnaces. Atmosphere furnaces are designed to operate with a controlled atmosphere, such as nitrogen, hydrogen, or argon, to prevent oxidation and ensure superior product quality. They are commonly used for processes like carburizing, annealing, and hardening. On the other hand, vacuum furnaces create a low-pressure environment by removing air and gases. This enables processes such as brazing, sintering, and heat treating, as the absence of oxygen prevents reactions and contamination. Both types offer specialized environments for different heat treatment applications.</p></p>
