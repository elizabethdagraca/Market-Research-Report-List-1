<p><p>The glass fiber prepreg market consists of different types such as epoxy resin system and phenolic resin system. Epoxy resin system refers to the use of epoxy resin as the matrix material in the glass fiber prepreg. Epoxy resin offers excellent mechanical properties, chemical resistance, and adhesion, making it suitable for various applications such as aerospace, automotive, and construction industries. On the other hand, phenolic resin system utilizes phenolic resin as the matrix material, which provides high heat resistance and fire retardancy properties, making it suitable for applications in the electrical and electronics industry.</p></p>
