<p><p>The Transportation Vehicle Automated Passenger Counting (APC) System market comprises three types: Infrared Type, Time-of-Flight Type, and Stereoscopic Vision Type. The Infrared Type uses infrared sensors to detect passengers entering and exiting vehicles. The Time-of-Flight Type measures the time taken for light to travel to and from passengers, providing accurate data. The Stereoscopic Vision Type uses cameras to capture three-dimensional images, allowing for precise passenger analysis. These APC systems help transportation providers collect real-time passenger data, aiding in route planning, resource allocation, and enhancing passenger safety and satisfaction.</p></p>
