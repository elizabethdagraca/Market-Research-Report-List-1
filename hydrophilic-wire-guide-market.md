<p><p>The hydrophilic wire guide market offers two main types: angled tip and straight tip. On the other hand, straight tip wire guides have a straight end, providing stability and control during simpler interventions. Both types are designed to be hydrophilic, meaning they have a special coating that enhances lubricity, minimizing friction and resistance, and ensuring smoother procedures.</p></p>
