<p><p>Inspection lamps are available in two market types: magnetic and non-magnetic. Magnetic inspection lamps are designed with a magnetic base which allows them to be easily attached to metallic surfaces, offering convenience and stability during inspections. On the other hand, non-magnetic inspection lamps lack the magnetic base and rely on other mechanisms or clamps to secure their position. Both types serve the purpose of providing lighting during inspections, but the choice between magnetic and non-magnetic depends on the specific requirements and the surfaces on which they will be used.</p></p>
