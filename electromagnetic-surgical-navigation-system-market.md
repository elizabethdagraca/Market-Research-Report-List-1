<p><p>The Electromagnetic Surgical Navigation System market comprises different types, with two common ones being Hybrid Navigation and Electromagnetic navigation. Hybrid Navigation systems combine the use of both preoperative imaging data and real-time patient tracking, providing surgeons with accurate guidance during procedures. On the other hand, Electromagnetic navigation systems rely on the interaction between electromagnetic fields and a sensor in the patient's body to navigate surgical instruments. These systems enhance precision and reduce invasiveness in various medical procedures.</p></p>
