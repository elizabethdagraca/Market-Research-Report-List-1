<p><p>Metro Ethernet Services are a type of high-speed data networking services that are typically provided within a metropolitan area. E-Line services refer to point-to-point connections that allow for the dedicated transmission of data between two locations. E-Access services provide connectivity to customers from their premises to the service provider's network. E-Transit services enable data traffic to pass through a service provider's network to connect multiple customer locations. Other market types may include services such as Virtual Private LAN Service (VPLS) or Ethernet LANs (ELANs) that offer additional features or variations of the previously mentioned services.</p></p>
