<p><p>The semiconductor X-ray defect inspection market comprises three types: X-ray Diffraction Imaging, Broadband Plasma Pattern, and Electron Beam Pattern. X-ray Diffraction Imaging is a technique that uses X-ray diffraction to analyze the crystal lattice structures for defects. Broadband Plasma Pattern uses plasma illumination to detect defects in the semiconductor material. Electron Beam Pattern uses an electron beam to scan and analyze the surface of the semiconductor for defects. These various methods are employed in the market to ensure the quality and reliability of semiconductor products.</p></p>
