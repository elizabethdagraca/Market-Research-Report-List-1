<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The LCD digitizer market has three main types: passive digitizer, active digitizer, and hybrid digitizer. A passive digitizer is a touch screen that relies on external forces (e.g., finger or stylus) to recognize input, making it less accurate but more affordable. An active digitizer, on the other hand, uses a special pen or stylus that emits signals to perform highly accurate touch recognition. Lastly, a hybrid digitizer combines both passive and active capabilities, providing a balance between accuracy and affordability. These three types cater to different user needs and preferences within the LCD digitizer market.</p></p>
