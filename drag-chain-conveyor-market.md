<p><p>Drag chain conveyors are a type of material handling equipment used for transporting bulk materials in various industries. Standard drag chain conveyors feature a series of connected chain links that drag the materials along the conveyor. On the other hand, en-masse drag chain conveyors have a deeper trough and use a larger amount of material to convey the bulk materials. They offer higher capacity and increased efficiency compared to standard drag chain conveyors. Both types of conveyors are widely used for their reliability and effectiveness in moving bulk materials.</p></p>
