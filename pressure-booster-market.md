<p><p>The pressure booster market is divided into different types based on the technology and functionality of the booster systems. Volumetric pressure boosters increase pressure by using a reciprocating pump or a piston, suitable for applications where low flow rates and high pressures are required. Centrifugal pressure boosters employ a rotating impeller to increase pressure and are ideal for high flow rate applications. Additionally, the market includes other types of boosters such as diaphragm and multistage boosters, designed to cater to specific industry requirements and individual applications.</p></p>
