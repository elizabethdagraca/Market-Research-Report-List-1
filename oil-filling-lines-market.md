<p><p>The oil filling lines market consists of two main types of machines: Fully-automatic machines and Semi-automatic machines. Fully-automatic machines are designed to perform the entire oil filling process without human intervention. These machines are equipped with advanced technology to automatically measure, fill, and seal the oil containers. On the other hand, Semi-automatic machines require some level of manual intervention. Although they automate certain processes like filling and capping, they still require human operators to control and monitor the overall operation. Both types of machines cater to the oil industry's diverse needs for efficient and convenient oil filling processes.</p></p>
