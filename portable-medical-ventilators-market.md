<p><p>Portable Medical Ventilators are medical devices used to assist the breathing process of patients who are unable to breathe on their own. Manual ventilators are operated manually by a healthcare professional, while mechanical ventilators use automated systems to deliver breaths to the patient. Manual ventilators are simpler and less expensive, making them suitable for basic respiratory support. On the other hand, mechanical ventilators are more complex and versatile, capable of delivering precise and adjustable breaths, making them suitable for intensive care settings. Both types of ventilators play a crucial role in providing respiratory assistance to patients in need.</p></p>
