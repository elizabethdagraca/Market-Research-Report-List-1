<p><p>The optical plastic lens market comprises two major types: spherical lenses and aspherical lenses. Spherical lenses have a curved surface, similar to a portion of a sphere, which helps in correcting vision problems like nearsightedness or farsightedness. They are commonly used in eyeglasses and camera lenses. On the other hand, aspherical lenses have a more complex surface, enabling them to correct additional optical aberrations and provide better image quality. They are widely used in high-end camera lenses, microscopes, and telescopes. Both types of lenses play a crucial role in various optical applications.</p></p>
