<p><p>The Digital Body Temperature Monitor market comprises three main types: digital thermometers, infrared thermometers, and other thermometers. Digital thermometers offer accurate readings by measuring body temperature through the mouth, armpit, or rectum. Infrared thermometers are contactless and provide fast results by reading the infrared heat emitted by the body. Other thermometers include wearable devices, such as smartwatches or patches, that continuously monitor body temperature. These types of devices have gained popularity due to their convenience, accuracy, and ability to connect with mobile apps for better health tracking.</p></p>
