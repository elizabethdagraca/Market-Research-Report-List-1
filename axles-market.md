<p><p>Axles are crucial components of vehicles that facilitate the rotation of wheels. There are three main types of axles in the market: live, dead, and tandem axles. Live axles are directly connected to the engine and transmit power to the wheels, resulting in better traction. Dead axles are not powered and simply support the weight of the vehicle. Tandem axles refer to a pair of axles that are closely positioned to distribute the weight of heavy-duty vehicles more evenly, allowing for increased load capacity and stability.</p></p>
