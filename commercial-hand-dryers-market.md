<p><p>The commercial hand dryers market consists of two types: jet air hand dryers and hot air hand dryers. Jet air hand dryers use high-speed airflow to quickly dry hands. They are considered more hygienic as they use a filtration system to eliminate bacteria. On the other hand, hot air hand dryers blow warm air onto hands for drying purposes. They are less expensive than jet air dryers but take longer to dry hands. Both types are commonly found in commercial settings such as restrooms and are designed to provide a convenient and efficient hand drying solution.</p></p>
