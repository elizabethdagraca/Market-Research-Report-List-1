The increasing adoption of cloud-based services, the proliferation of IoT devices, and the growing need for efficient management of IP addresses are the key drivers of market growth.</p><p>One of the major trends in the DNS, DHCP, IP Address Management (DDI) Market is the integration of DDI solutions with network security tools. With the rising number of cybersecurity threats, organizations are focusing on strengthening their network security infrastructure. By integrating DDI solutions with security tools, companies can gain enhanced visibility, control, and protection over their network traffic.</p><p>Another trend is the adoption of virtual DDI solutions. Virtual DDI solutions offer flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness by eliminating the need for dedicated hardware. These solutions enable organizations to manage their IP addresses, DNS, and DHCP services in a virtualized environment.</p><p>Additionally, there is a growing demand for DDI solutions that support IPv6.
