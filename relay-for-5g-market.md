<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Relay</li><li>SSR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Relay for 5G market primarily comprises two types, electromagnetic relay and solid-state relay (SSR). Electromagnetic relay functions by using an electromagnet to control one or several switch contacts, allowing signals to be processed or transmitted. On the other hand, SSR employs semiconductors, eliminating the need for mechanical parts. SSR is preferred in 5G networks due to its high switching speed, compact design, and better resistance to vibration and shock. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring smooth and efficient communication in 5G networks by controlling the flow of signals.</p></p>
