<p><p>Rubber moulding machines are used to shape rubber materials and are available in two main types: automatic and manual. They are more efficient and designed for large-scale production. On the other hand, manual machines require human operation and supervision throughout the moulding process. They are suitable for small-scale or customized production, where manual control and flexibility are important.</p></p>
