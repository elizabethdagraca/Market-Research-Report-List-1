The market for PET Collection Tubes can be categorized into different types: Serum Separating Tubes, EDTA Tubes, Plasma Separation Tubes, and Others. Serum Separating Tubes are designed to separate the serum from the blood, whereas EDTA Tubes are used for whole blood collection and preservation. These tubes play a crucial role in the accurate analysis and diagnosis of various medical conditions.</p></p>
