<p><p>The saw wire market consists of two major types: electroplated wire and resin wire. Electroplated wire is a high-performance cutting tool used in various industries. It offers excellent cutting efficiency and long lifespan due to its diamond particles electroplated onto the wire. On the other hand, resin wire is a cost-effective alternative commonly used in less demanding applications. It is made by coating diamond particles onto a steel core using resin binder, providing efficient cutting with a relatively shorter lifespan. Both types cater to different needs and industries.</p></p>
