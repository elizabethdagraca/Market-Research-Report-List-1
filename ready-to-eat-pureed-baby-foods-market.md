<p><p>Ready-to-Eat Pureed Baby Foods come in three different stages. Stage 1 Pureed Baby Foods are intended for infants who are just starting to eat solid foods. They consist of simple single-ingredient purees, such as fruits and vegetables. Stage 2 Pureed Baby Foods are designed for babies who have moved past single-ingredient purees and are ready for more complex flavors and combinations. These foods often include meat, grains, and multi-ingredient blends. Stage 3 Pureed Baby Foods are for older babies who are becoming more adept at eating and can handle thicker textures. These foods may include chunks of food or small lumps to encourage chewing and self-feeding.</p></p>
