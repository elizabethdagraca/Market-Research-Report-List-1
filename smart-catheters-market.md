<p><p>Smart catheters are advanced medical devices used for various diagnostic and treatment procedures in interventional cardiology, urology, and neurology. Single lumen catheters consist of a single hollow tube, allowing for the insertion and drainage of fluids or medications. On the other hand, multi-lumen catheters have multiple channels or lumens, enabling multiple functions such as simultaneous fluid drainage and pressure monitoring. Both types of catheters are equipped with smart features like sensors, wireless connectivity, and real-time monitoring capabilities, enhancing their effectiveness in patient care and improving healthcare outcomes.</p></p>
