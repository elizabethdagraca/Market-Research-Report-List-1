<p><strong>The ALD Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PE-ALD</li><li>Thermal ALD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ALD (Atomic Layer Deposition) Systems encompass a variety of market types, including PE-ALD and Thermal ALD. PE-ALD refers to Plasma-Enhanced ALD, which utilizes plasma to enhance the deposition process of thin films, allowing for precise and controlled growth. On the other hand, Thermal ALD involves using heat to facilitate the deposition of thin films and is known for its ability to deposit high-quality and uniform films. Both market types serve different purposes and cater to diverse industries, such as semiconductors, electronics, and photovoltaics.</p></p>
