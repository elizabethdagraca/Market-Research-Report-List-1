<p><p>The CBRN Defence Market includes three types: CBRN Passive Protection, Pollution Avoidance, and CBRN Mitigation. CBRN Passive Protection refers to measures that prevent or minimize the impact of CBRN threats without actively engaging them, such as protective clothing and ventilation systems. Pollution Avoidance involves activities that aim to detect and avoid potential sources of chemical, biological, radiological, or nuclear contamination before they cause harm. CBRN Mitigation focuses on actions taken after a CBRN incident to minimize its impact, including decontamination and medical treatment.</p></p>
