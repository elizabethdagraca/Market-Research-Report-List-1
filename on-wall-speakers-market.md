<p><p>The on-wall speakers market is divided into two main types: wired and wireless. Wired speakers require speaker wires to connect to an audio source or amplifier, providing a stable and reliable connection. They are typically used in permanent installations where the speaker placement remains constant. On the other hand, wireless speakers utilize wireless technology such as Bluetooth or Wi-Fi to transmit audio signals, allowing for more flexibility in terms of placement and easy connectivity with different devices. This makes them suitable for portable and versatile applications.</p></p>
