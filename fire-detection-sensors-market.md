<p><p>Fire detection sensors are devices used to detect and respond to the presence of fire. This market can be categorized into two types: wired and wireless. Wired fire detection sensors rely on physical connections to transmit signals and data, necessitating a structured network. On the other hand, wireless fire detection sensors use radio frequency technology to transmit data to a control panel or monitoring system without the need for physical connections. Both wired and wireless sensors play a crucial role in fire safety by detecting and alerting individuals to the presence of fire.</p></p>
