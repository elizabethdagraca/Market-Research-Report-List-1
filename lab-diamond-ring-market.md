<p><p>The lab diamond ring market includes two main types: CVD diamonds and HTHP diamonds. CVD diamonds are created through a chemical vapor deposition process, wherein a diamond is grown from a carbon-rich gas onto a substrate. This method allows for the production of high-quality diamonds with distinct clarity characteristics. On the other hand, HTHP diamonds are produced using high pressure and high temperature techniques to recreate the natural diamond formation process. Both types of lab diamonds cater to consumers looking for ethically sourced and sustainable alternatives to mined diamonds.</p></p>
