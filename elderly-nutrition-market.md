<p><strong>The Elderly Nutrition Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parenteral Elderly Nutrition</li><li>Enteral Elderly Nutrition</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Elderly nutrition market consists of parenteral and enteral nutrition options. Parenteral elderly nutrition involves the administration of nutrients directly into the bloodstream through intravenous means. This method is used when the digestive system is not functioning properly. Enteral elderly nutrition, on the other hand, refers to the delivery of nutrients through a feeding tube that goes directly into the stomach or small intestine. This type of nutrition is implemented when the elderly individual cannot consume enough food orally or has difficulty swallowing. Both parenteral and enteral nutrition options are vital in catering to the specific dietary needs of the elderly population.</p></p>
