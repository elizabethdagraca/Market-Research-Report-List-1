<p><p>Fans and blowers are essential equipment used to circulate air or gas in industrial and commercial settings. Axial fans and blowers are designed to move air or gas parallel to the axis of rotation, generating high airflow at low pressure. Centrifugal fans and blowers, on the other hand, work by accelerating air or gas radially outward, producing higher pressure at a lower airflow rate. Other types of fans and blowers may include cross-flow fans, mixed flow fans, and regenerative blowers, each with their unique characteristics and applications.</p></p>
