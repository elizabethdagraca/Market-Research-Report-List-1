<p><p>The bronchoscope video endoscopes market includes two main types: rigid endoscopes and flexible endoscopes. Rigid endoscopes are straight and inflexible, often used when a clear image is required. They are commonly used for diagnostic procedures. On the other hand, flexible endoscopes are thinner, longer, and bendable. They allow for easier access to hard-to-reach areas and can be used for both diagnostic and therapeutic purposes.
