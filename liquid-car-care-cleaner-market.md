<p><p>The Liquid Car Care Cleaner market consists of two major types: Car External Cleaner and Car Internal Cleaner. Car External Cleaners are products specifically designed to clean the exterior surfaces of vehicles, removing dirt, grime, and other contaminants. On the other hand, Car Internal Cleaners are used to clean the interior surfaces of cars, including seats, dashboard, carpets, and upholstery.
