<p><p>Venturi ejectors are widely used in various industries, including marine, chemical, and oil and gas. The market for Venturi ejectors can be categorized into three types: single stage ejectors, two stage ejectors, and other markets. </p><p>Single stage ejectors utilize one venturi section to create suction and amplify the fluid flow, making them suitable for applications with low to medium pressure differentials. Two stage ejectors, on the other hand, consist of two venturi sections, allowing for even greater suction and amplification, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. </p><p>The "other" market includes different types of ejectors, such as multi-stage ejectors or special purpose ejectors, which cater to specific industry requirements and niche applications.</p></p>
