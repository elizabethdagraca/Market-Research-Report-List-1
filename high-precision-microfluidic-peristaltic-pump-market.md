<p><p>A high precision microfluidic peristaltic pump is a device used in the field of microfluidics to accurately control the flow rate of small volumes of liquids. It operates based on the peristaltic principle, where a series of rollers compresses and relaxes a flexible tube, creating a wave-like motion that propels the liquid forward. These pumps are widely used in various applications such as drug discovery, analytical chemistry, genomics, and proteomics.</p><p>The high precision microfluidic peristaltic pump market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The rise in demand for microfluidic devices and the increasing adoption of lab-on-a-chip technology are the primary factors driving the market growth.
