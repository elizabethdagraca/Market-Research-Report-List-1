<p><p>Silicon photomultipliers (SiPMs) are capable of detecting low-level light signals and have various applications in industries like medical imaging, nuclear physics, and environmental monitoring. The market for SiPMs can be categorized into two types: analog and digital. Analog SiPMs convert incoming photons into an analog electrical current, allowing for continuous signal measurement. Digital SiPMs, on the other hand, transform light into discrete electrical pulses, enabling direct digital counting and providing higher resolution. These different types cater to the specific needs of different industries while leveraging the unique capabilities of SiPM technology.</p></p>
