CNS focuses on communication and navigation technologies that ensure efficient connectivity and safe flights. FCS involves systems that enable control over an aircraft's flight and movements. FMS helps in automated management of flight operations, enhancing efficiency and precision. Lastly, the IFE market pertains to entertainment systems integrated into aircraft cabins, offering passengers various multimedia options to enhance their onboard experience.</p></p>
