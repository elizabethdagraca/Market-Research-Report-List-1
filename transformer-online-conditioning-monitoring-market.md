<p><strong>The Transformer Online Conditioning Monitoring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DGA</li><li>Partial Discharge (PD)</li><li>Bushing Monitoring</li><li>Through Fault Current</li><li>Integrated Online Monitoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Transformer Online Conditioning Monitoring market includes various types of monitoring techniques. Dissolved Gas Analysis (DGA) is used to identify and analyze gases dissolved in transformer oil, indicating potential faults. Partial Discharge (PD) monitoring focuses on detecting small electrical discharges within insulation materials. Bushing monitoring involves assessing the condition of transformer bushings, essential for insulation and connection. Through Fault Current monitoring helps identify the impact of fault currents on transformer components. Finally, Integrated Online Monitoring combines multiple techniques to provide a comprehensive assessment of a transformer's condition.</p></p>
