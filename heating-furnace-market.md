<p><p>The heating furnace market can be classified into two main types: combustion type and electric type. In combustion type furnaces, fuel such as gas, oil, or coal is burned to produce heat. These furnaces are commonly used in areas with easy access to fuel sources. On the other hand, electric type furnaces use electricity as their source of heat. They are preferred in areas where fuel sources are limited or where electricity is more affordable. Both types aim to provide efficient and effective heating solutions for various residential, commercial, and industrial applications.</p></p>
