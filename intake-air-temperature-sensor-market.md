<p><p>The Intake Air Temperature Sensor is available in three market types. The first type is integrated into the Manifold Absolute Pressure Sensor, which measures both pressure and temperature. The second type is the Standalone Intake-Air Temperature Sensor, which is solely dedicated to measuring air temperature. The third type is integrated into the Mass Air Flow Sensor, which measures both the amount of air entering the engine and its temperature. Each type serves a specific purpose in providing accurate data for engine performance and fuel efficiency.</p></p>
