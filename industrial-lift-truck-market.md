<p><p>The industrial lift truck market is categorized into three main types: Class 1, Class 2, and Class 3. </p><p>Class 1 includes electric motor rider trucks, which are designed for outdoor and indoor use. These trucks are powered by electricity and primarily used in warehouses and manufacturing facilities.</p><p>Class 2 comprises electric motor narrow aisle trucks used for indoor material handling. These trucks are specifically designed to operate in narrow spaces, such as narrow aisles in warehouses.</p><p>Class 3 represents electric motor hand trucks or walk-behind trucks. These trucks are typically used for shorter distances and lighter loads, offering manual or electric lift capabilities for convenience and efficiency.</p></p>
