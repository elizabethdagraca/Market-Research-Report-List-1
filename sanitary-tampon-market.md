Women are becoming more conscious about the materials used in such products, leading to an increased demand for tampons made from organic cotton or other natural fibers. Secondly, the rise in menstrual cup usage as an alternative to traditional tampons has impacted market dynamics. Menstrual cups are reusable, eco-friendly, and cost-effective, offering women an alternative choice for their menstrual hygiene needs.</p><p>Overall, the sanitary tampon market is expected to witness significant growth due to factors such as increasing awareness, product innovation, and changing consumer preferences towards sustainable and organic feminine hygiene solutions.</p></p>
