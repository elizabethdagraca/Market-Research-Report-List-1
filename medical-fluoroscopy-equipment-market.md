<p><p>Medical fluoroscopy equipment refers to imaging devices used in medical settings to obtain real-time X-ray images of a patient's internal structures. One type is fixed fluoroscopy equipment, which is permanently installed in a specific location within a medical facility. It is commonly used for procedures requiring prolonged imaging, such as angiography. Another type is C-Arms, which are mobile and can be easily moved around the patient. C-Arms are commonly used during surgical and interventional procedures, providing real-time imaging guidance to surgeons or physicians. Both types of equipment play a crucial role in diagnostic and interventional procedures in medical settings.</p></p>
