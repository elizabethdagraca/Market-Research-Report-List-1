The market includes different types of valves like Butterfly Valve and Ball Valve. The Butterfly Valve consists of a disk that rotates to control the flow and pressure. It is typically lightweight and used for larger pipes. On the other hand, the Ball Valve uses a spherical ball with a hole in it to control the flow and pressure. It is commonly used for smaller pipes due to its compact design.</p></p>
