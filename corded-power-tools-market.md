<p><p>Corded power tools are electric tools that require a direct power source to operate. These tools are widely used in various industries such as construction, manufacturing, and automotive for tasks that require high power and consistent performance. Unlike cordless power tools, which rely on batteries for operation, corded power tools offer unlimited runtime and do not require regular recharging.</p><p>The market for corded power tools has been witnessing significant growth in recent years. One of the key factors driving this growth is the increasing demand for power tools in construction activities worldwide. The construction industry is experiencing robust growth, particularly in developing countries, due to rapid urbanization and infrastructure development. As corded power tools are essential for tasks such as drilling, cutting, and fastening in construction projects, the market for these tools is expected to expand.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in technology have led to the development of corded power tools with enhanced features and performance. Manufacturers are introducing power tools with improved ergonomics, increased power output, and higher durability to cater to the evolving needs of end-users. Additionally, the growing trend of do-it-yourself (DIY) projects among consumers is also contributing to the demand for corded power tools.</p><p>According to the forecast, the corded power tools market is projected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 13.9% during the forecast period. Additionally, the rising adoption of power tools in emerging markets is expected to further boost the market growth.</p><p>In conclusion, the corded power tools market is expected to witness substantial growth in the coming years, driven by the expanding construction industry, technological advancements, and increasing consumer preference for DIY projects.
