<p><p>The small power transformer market below 220kV is categorized into two types: dry-type transformers and oil-immersed transformers. Dry-type transformers use solid insulation materials, making them safe and maintenance-free. These transformers are commonly used in indoor applications that have fire safety requirements. On the other hand, oil-immersed transformers are filled with oil as a cooling and insulation medium. They usually find application in outdoor installations due to their high efficiency and ability to handle fluctuating loads. Both types cater to the demand for small power transformers below 220kV.</p></p>
