<p><strong>The Medical Printing Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Film</li><li>Thermal Film</li><li>B-ultrasound Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical printing film market consists of three main types: dry film, thermal film, and B-ultrasound film. Dry film is a type of film that does not require any processing chemicals and can be used directly for printing medical images. Thermal film, on the other hand, uses a heat-sensitive coating to produce images when heat is applied. Lastly, B-ultrasound film is specifically designed for printing ultrasound images. These different types of films serve various purposes in the medical field and contribute to the efficient printing of medical images.</p></p>
