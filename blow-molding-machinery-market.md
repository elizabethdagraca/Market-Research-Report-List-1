<p><p>Blow molding machinery market is categorized into several types. Streamline plastic machinery refers to the equipment used for manufacturing plastic products using the streamline process. Injection molding machinery is utilized for shaping plastic materials by injecting them into a mold. Blow molding machinery is employed for creating hollow plastic products by inflating a heated plastic tube in a mold. Thermoforming machinery is used to produce plastic products by heating and then shaping plastic sheets. Lastly, the "others" category encompasses various types of machinery not included in the aforementioned categories.</p></p>
