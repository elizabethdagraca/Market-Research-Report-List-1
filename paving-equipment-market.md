<p><ul><li>Mechanical Pavers</li><li>Hydrostatic Pavers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Paving equipment market types include mechanical pavers and hydrostatic pavers. Mechanical pavers employ an internal combustion engine to generate mechanical power, which is then used to operate the machine and lay the asphalt or concrete. On the other hand, hydrostatic pavers rely on hydraulic systems for their power and function. These systems use pressurized liquid to transmit forces, allowing for more precise control and smoother operation. Both types of pavers are utilized in the construction industry for efficient and reliable road or pavement construction.</p></p>
