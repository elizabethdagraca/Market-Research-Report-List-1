<p><p>The sigmoidoscope market consists of two main types: flexible sigmoidoscope and rigid sigmoidoscope. Flexible sigmoidoscopes are long, flexible tubes with a light and camera at the end, allowing doctors to visually examine the sigmoid colon. They are usually preferred for their ease of use and patient comfort. Rigid sigmoidoscopes, on the other hand, are inflexible devices with a straight shape, providing a more rigid and direct visualization of the lower colon. Both types are used in medical procedures to diagnose and treat conditions such as rectal bleeding and polyps.</p></p>
