<p><ul><li>Onboard</li><li>On Ground</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Health Monitoring market consists of two types: Onboard and On Ground. </p><p>Onboard monitoring refers to real-time monitoring systems installed within the aircraft, collecting data during the flight. These systems enable in-flight diagnostics and provide immediate alerts for any potential issues or abnormalities. They assist in proactive maintenance, reducing downtime and increasing operational efficiency.</p><p>On the other hand, On Ground monitoring involves analyzing data collected from the aircraft while it is on the ground. This includes data from previous flights and is used for maintenance planning, analysis of trends, and identifying long-term operational improvements.</p></p>
