<p><ul><li>Centralized Automated Dispensing Systems</li><li>De-Centralized Automated Dispensing Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Centralized automated dispensing systems refer to a type of automatic dispensing machine that is located in a centralized area such as a hospital or pharmacy. These systems are controlled by a central computer and are designed to store and dispense a wide range of medications securely and efficiently. On the other hand, de-centralized automated dispensing systems are smaller machines that are located throughout a facility, allowing healthcare professionals quick access to specific medications without having to travel to a central location. Both types of systems enhance medication management and improve patient safety.</p></p>
