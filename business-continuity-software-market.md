<p><p>Business continuity software is a solution that helps organizations recover and restore critical processes during disruptive events. The market can be categorized into two types: Cloud-Based and On-Premise. Cloud-Based software is hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, allowing flexibility, scalability, and remote accessibility, but dependent on internet connectivity. On the other hand, On-Premise software is installed directly on the company's servers, offering more control and data security, but requiring maintenance and infrastructure investment.
