<p><p>A portable space heater is a device used to heat a small area or room. There are various types of portable space heaters available in the market. Convector heaters work by circulating air through the unit, which heats up the surrounding space. They are known for providing a consistent and even heat distribution. On the other hand, radiant heaters emit infrared radiation that directly warms objects and people in the vicinity. These heaters are known for providing instant heat and are often preferred for localized heating. Both types cater to different heating preferences and purposes in the market.</p></p>
