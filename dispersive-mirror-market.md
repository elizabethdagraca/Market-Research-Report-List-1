<p><ul><li>Gires-Tournois Interferometer (GTI) Mirror</li><li>Chirped Mirror</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dispersive Mirror market consists of two prominent types: Gires-Tournois Interferometer (GTI) Mirror and Chirped Mirror. GTI mirror is a device that reflects different colors of light at different angles, allowing it to separate and manipulate light waves with high precision. On the other hand, Chirped Mirror is specifically designed to compensate for dispersion in ultrafast optics by varying its reflectivity along its surface. Both types of mirrors are crucial in fields like telecommunications, lasers, and spectroscopy for controlling light properties and achieving desired outcomes.</p></p>
