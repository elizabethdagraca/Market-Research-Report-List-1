<p><p>The Ti Sapphire Laser market consists of three main types: Mode-locked Oscillators, Chirped-pulse Amplifiers, and Tunable Continuous Wave Lasers. Mode-locked Oscillators generate ultra-fast laser pulses with extremely short durations. Chirped-pulse Amplifiers are used to amplify these pulses to higher energy levels while maintaining their short durations. Tunable Continuous Wave Lasers offer wide tunability in their output wavelength, providing flexibility for various applications. Each type serves distinct requirements within the market, catering to diverse needs for ultrafast, high-energy, and tunable lasers.</p></p>
