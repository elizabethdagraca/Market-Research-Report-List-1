<p><p>The medical nebulizer market consists of three main types: pneumatic nebulizers, ultrasonic nebulizers, and mesh nebulizers. Pneumatic nebulizers work by using compressed air or oxygen to convert liquid medication into a fine mist for inhalation. Ultrasonic nebulizers use high-frequency sound waves to generate aerosol droplets. Mesh nebulizers incorporate a vibrating mesh to break up the medication into tiny particles. These different types offer various advantages in terms of efficiency, convenience, and ease of use, catering to the diverse needs of patients requiring respiratory medication.</p></p>
