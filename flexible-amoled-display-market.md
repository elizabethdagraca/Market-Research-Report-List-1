<p><strong>The Flexible AMOLED Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode(AMOLED Display)</li><li>Passive Matrix OLED(PMOLED)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flexible AMOLED Display Market Types refer to the different display technologies that are used in flexible OLED screens. Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode (AMOLED Display) is a highly advanced technology that offers high-resolution, vibrant colors, and faster refresh rates. It is commonly used in smartphones and high-end displays. Passive Matrix OLED (PMOLED) is a simpler and cheaper technology suitable for smaller or lower-resolution devices. PMOLED offers lower power consumption and better visibility in bright conditions but lacks the performance and color accuracy of AMOLED. Both technologies play a significant role in the flexible display market.</p></p>
