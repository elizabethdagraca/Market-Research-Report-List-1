<p><p>The vacuum blood collection system market typologies include various types of tubes used for collecting blood samples. Gel tubes contain a gel at the bottom that separates serum from blood cells, making it suitable for tests like biochemistry. Heparin tubes contain heparin anticoagulant and are used for tests requiring plasma. EDTA tubes contain an anticoagulant called EDTA and are ideal for hematology tests. Serum tubes lack any additives and are specifically designed for serum collection. The "others" category comprises additional tubes with different additives like citrate, fluoride, or oxalate used for specific tests.</p></p>
