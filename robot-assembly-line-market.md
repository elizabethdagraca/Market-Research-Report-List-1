<p><p>The Robot Assembly Line market consists of three main types: Six-axis robots, Four-axis robots, and Collaborative robots. </p><p>Six-axis robots are versatile machines equipped with six degrees of freedom, enabling them to perform complex tasks precisely and efficiently. They are commonly used in industries such as automotive and electronics for assembly, material handling, and welding.</p><p>Four-axis robots, on the other hand, have four degrees of freedom and are simpler in design. They are often used for basic assembly tasks that require less flexibility.</p><p>Collaborative robots, also known as cobots, are a newer addition to the market. These robots are designed to work alongside humans, promoting teamwork and enhancing productivity. They are equipped with advanced safety features to ensure a safe working environment.</p></p>
