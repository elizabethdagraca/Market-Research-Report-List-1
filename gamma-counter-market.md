<p><p>Gamma counters are used in nuclear medicine laboratories to measure radioactivity in samples. There are three types of gamma counters in the market. Automatic gamma counters are fully automated devices that perform all the necessary functions, from sample preparation to data analysis. Semi-automatic gamma counters assist with sample preparation but require manual data input. Other types of gamma counters refer to manual devices that rely on the user for both sample preparation and data analysis. The choice of gamma counter depends on the level of automation desired and the specific needs of the laboratory.</p></p>
