Unaided systems do not require the use of any external tools or equipment and rely solely on the individual's body movements, gestures, or sign language. Aided systems, on the other hand, involve the use of external devices such as communication boards, computers, or tablets that help individuals communicate effectively. These devices range from simple picture boards to sophisticated technology-based solutions.</p></p>
