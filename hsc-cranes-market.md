<p><p>HSC Cranes are available in several market types. Single girder cranes feature a single bridge beam and are suitable for lighter loads. Double girder bridge cranes have two parallel bridge beams, allowing for increased lifting capacity and stability. Gantry cranes are versatile and can be used in outdoor environments. Monorail cranes operate on a single rail and are ideal for transporting materials along a fixed path. Each type of crane has its advantages and applications, catering to various user requirements in the market.</p></p>
