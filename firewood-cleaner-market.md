<p><strong>The Firewood Cleaner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drum</li><li>Roller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The firewood cleaner market offers two main types of products: drum and roller cleaners. Drum cleaners are designed with a rotating cylindrical drum that agitates the firewood, removing dirt, debris, and bark. They are typically more affordable and easier to operate. On the other hand, roller cleaners consist of a series of rollers that exert pressure on the firewood, achieving a thorough cleaning. They are often considered more efficient but can be more expensive and require more maintenance. Both types cater to the demand for effective firewood cleaning, with drum cleaners being more accessible and roller cleaners offering higher performance.</p></p>
