<p><p>Airborne Molecular Contamination (AMC) Monitors are devices used to monitor and measure the level of molecular contamination in the air. There are two main types of AMC monitors: multi-point sampling systems and single-point sampling systems. </p><p>Multi-point sampling systems allow for simultaneous monitoring at various locations within a facility, providing a comprehensive assessment of AMC levels. This type of monitor is suitable for larger environments with multiple potential contamination sources.</p><p>On the other hand, single-point sampling systems focus on monitoring a specific location or critical area. These monitors are often used in smaller spaces or areas of high importance where targeted monitoring is necessary.</p></p>
