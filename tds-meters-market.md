<p><p>TDS meters are devices used to measure the total dissolved solids in a liquid. There are two main types of TDS meters in the market: portable TDS meters and benchtop TDS meters. </p><p>Portable TDS meters are compact, handheld devices that can easily be carried around. They are commonly used for fieldwork, quick water quality testing, and on-the-spot TDS measurements. Portable TDS meters are suitable for both personal and professional use due to their convenience and ease of use. </p><p>On the other hand, benchtop TDS meters are larger, more advanced instruments designed for laboratory and industrial settings. They are typically placed on a bench or table and offer more accurate and detailed readings compared to portable meters. Benchtop TDS meters are commonly used in scientific research, water treatment plants, and manufacturing facilities where precise measurements are crucial.</p></p>
