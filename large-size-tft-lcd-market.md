<p><p>The large-size TFT-LCD market consists of various types including TN (Twisted Nematic), IPS (In-Plane Switching), AFFS (Advanced Fringe Field Switching), MVA (Multi-domain Vertical Alignment), and Others. TN displays are known for their fast response time, but have limited viewing angles. IPS screens offer superior color reproduction and wide viewing angles. AFFS panels provide high contrast, visibility, and are suitable for outdoor environments. MVA displays deliver excellent color performance and wide viewing angles. "Others" refers to any other types of TFT-LCD screens that do not fall under the aforementioned categories.</p></p>
