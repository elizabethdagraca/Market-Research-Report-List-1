<p><p>PET Cyclotrons are compact particle accelerators that are commonly used in the production of radiopharmaceuticals for Positron Emission Tomography (PET) imaging. These machines accelerate protons to high speeds and then collide them with a target material, resulting in the production of short-lived radioisotopes. These radioisotopes can be conveniently incorporated into radiopharmaceuticals, which are then used in PET scans for the diagnosis and monitoring of various diseases, such as cancer and neurological disorders.</p><p>The PET Cyclotrons Market is anticipated to experience significant growth over the forecast period. Factors driving market growth include the increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, rising demand for accurate diagnostic techniques, advancements in radiopharmaceutical research, and the growing focus on personalized medicine.
