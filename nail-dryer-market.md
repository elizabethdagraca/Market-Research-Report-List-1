<p><p>The nail dryer market consists of two main types: UV/LED nail dryers and fan nail dryers. UV/LED nail dryers use ultraviolet or light-emitting diode technology to cure and dry nail polish quickly. They are more efficient and often preferred by professionals, but they may require additional steps or products. On the other hand, fan nail dryers blow cool air onto the nails, evaporating the polish. They are generally more affordable and easier to use, making them popular among individuals for home use.</p></p>
