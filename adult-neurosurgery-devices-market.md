<p><p>The adult neurosurgery devices market is categorized into two main types: neuromodulation devices and neuroendoscopy devices. Neuromodulation devices involve the use of electrical stimulation to target specific areas in the brain or spinal cord to alleviate symptoms of neurological disorders like Parkinson's disease or chronic pain. Neuroendoscopy devices, on the other hand, are used for minimally invasive procedures, enabling surgeons to visualize and access the brain or spinal cord using small incisions and endoscopes. These devices help improve patient outcomes by reducing the need for traditional open surgery and promoting faster recovery.</p></p>
