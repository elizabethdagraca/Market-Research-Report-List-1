<p><p>The machine vision objectives market consists of different types, including spherical objectives and aspheric objectives. Spherical objectives are lenses with a curved surface, enabling them to focus light to a single point and provide clear images. Aspheric objectives, on the other hand, have a more complex surface shape, allowing for better correction of aberrations and enhanced image quality. Both types are used in machine vision systems to capture accurate and detailed images for various applications such as quality control, measurement, and inspection processes.</p></p>
