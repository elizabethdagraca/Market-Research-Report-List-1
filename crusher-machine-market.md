<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The crusher machine market can be categorized into three types: jaw crusher, gyratory crusher, and cone crusher. A jaw crusher is the most commonly used primary crushing equipment that uses compressive force to crush materials. A gyratory crusher, on the other hand, uses a mantle and concave surfaces for crushing large materials. Lastly, a cone crusher operates by squeezing materials between an eccentrically rotating cone and a concave, producing finer particles. These machines are widely used in various industries for efficient material crushing.</p></p>
