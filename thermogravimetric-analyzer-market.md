<p><p>A Thermogravimetric Analyzer (TGA) is a high-precision instrument used to measure changes in the weight of a sample as a function of temperature or time, under controlled atmospheric conditions. It measures the weight loss or gain of a sample as it undergoes thermal decomposition, reactions, or desorption. TGA finds extensive applications in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, polymers, ceramics, and chemicals, due to its ability to determine precise data on sample composition, purity, and thermal stability.</p><p>The Thermogravimetric Analyzer Market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. <p><p>The Thermogravimetric Analyzer (TGA) market can be split into two types: High Pressure TGA and Atmospheric TGA. High Pressure TGA instruments are used to analyze materials in a controlled high-pressure environment, allowing for accurate measurement of weight change at various temperatures. On the other hand, Atmospheric TGA instruments operate at standard atmospheric conditions, making them suitable for analyzing materials without the need for controlled pressure. Both types of TGA have their applications and benefits, depending on the specific research or analysis requirements.</p></p>
