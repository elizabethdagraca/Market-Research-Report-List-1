<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>AC welding machines are categorized into three types: Moving Iron Core, Homo Style, and Moving Coil. Each type utilizes different mechanisms to generate AC voltage. Moving Iron Core machines have a fixed coil and a movable iron core, which helps in voltage regulation. Homo Style machines have an iron core attached to the movable coil, resulting in efficient voltage control. Moving Coil machines have a fixed coil and a movable coil, generating AC voltage through electromagnetic induction. These three types offer different advantages and applications in the AC welding machines market.</p></p>
