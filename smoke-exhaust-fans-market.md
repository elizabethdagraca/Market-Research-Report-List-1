<p><p>The smoke exhaust fans market consists of three main types: centrifugal type, axial type, and others. Centrifugal type fans are designed to move air radially outward from the center and are used in applications with higher pressure and air volume requirements. Axial type fans, on the other hand, move air parallel to the fan's axis and are ideal for low-pressure and high-volume applications. Lastly, the "others" category includes specialized fans such as mixed flow fans and tangential fans, which are used in specific situations that require unique airflow patterns.</p></p>
