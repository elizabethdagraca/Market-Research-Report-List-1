<p><p>Keratin tablets are a popular hair growth and strengthening supplement. They come in two market types: alpha-keratin and beta-keratin. Alpha-keratin is the most abundant protein in mammals and is responsible for the structure and strength of hair, nails, and skin. Beta-keratin, on the other hand, is found in reptiles, birds, and some mammals, and is primarily found in the structure of scales, feathers, and beaks. Both types of keratin tablets aim to provide essential amino acids and nutrients to promote healthy hair growth and improve overall hair health.</p></p>
