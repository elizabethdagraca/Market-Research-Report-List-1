<p><p>AC sealed contactors are electrical devices used for controlling electrical power circuits in various industrial applications. These contactors consist of a coil, which when energized, creates a magnetic field that attracts a contact arm, allowing the flow of electrical current. Once the coil is de-energized, the contact arm returns to its original position, breaking the circuit.</p><p>The global AC sealed contactor market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing need for efficient and reliable motor control systems in various industries is driving the demand for AC sealed contactors. These contactors offer advantages such as safety, durability, and easy installation, which further propel their market growth.</p><p>Another major factor contributing to the market growth is the rising adoption of automation in industrial processes.
