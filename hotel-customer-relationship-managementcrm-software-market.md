<p><p>Hotel Customer Relationship Management (CRM) software is available in two market types: Cloud Based and Premise Based. Cloud-based CRM software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. It offers easy scalability, flexibility, and lower upfront costs as it does not require hardware installation or maintenance. On the other hand, premise-based CRM software needs to be installed locally on the hotel's servers and is managed by the hotel's IT team. It offers greater control, data security, and customization options, but requires higher upfront investment and ongoing maintenance.</p></p>
