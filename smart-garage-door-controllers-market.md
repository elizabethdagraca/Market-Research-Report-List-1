<p><p>The Smart Garage Door Controllers market is divided into two main types: Wi-Fi-based and Bluetooth-based. Wi-Fi-based controllers enable users to connect and operate their garage doors remotely using their home's Wi-Fi network. It allows for seamless integration with other smart home devices and provides greater control and accessibility through smartphone apps. On the other hand, Bluetooth-based controllers use short-range wireless technology to establish a connection between the garage door and a user's mobile device. While not as versatile as Wi-Fi-based controllers, Bluetooth-based controllers offer a more cost-effective option for those looking for basic remote operation of their garage doors.</p></p>
