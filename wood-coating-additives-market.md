<p><p>Wood coating additives are crucial components that enhance the performance and properties of wood coatings. Dispersing agents aid in the dispersion of solid particles, ensuring a uniform finish. Rheology modifiers improve the flow and consistency of the coating. Wetting agents help in better adhesion of the coating to the substrate. Surface modifiers enhance the durability and resistance of the coating. Other additives cater to specific requirements such as anti-foaming agents, UV stabilizers, and anti-settling agents, further improving the overall performance of wood coatings.</p></p>
