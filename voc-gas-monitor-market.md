<p><p>VOC Gas Monitors are devices used to detect and monitor the presence of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) in the atmosphere. Two common types of VOC Gas Monitors are PID (Photoionization Detector) and Metal-oxide Semiconductor detectors. PID monitors use ultraviolet light to ionize VOC molecules, allowing for detection and measurement. Metal-oxide Semiconductor detectors use a sensor that changes its electrical resistance when exposed to VOCs. Both types of monitors are widely used in industries such as manufacturing, refineries, and environmental monitoring to ensure safety and compliance with regulations.</p></p>
