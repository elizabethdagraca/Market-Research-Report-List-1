<p><p>The Ophthalmic Femtosecond Laser System market can be categorized into two types: Full Femtosecond and Half Femtosecond. Full Femtosecond laser systems refer to devices that utilize femtosecond lasers for all stages of a surgical procedure, from creating corneal incisions to performing lens fragmentation. On the other hand, Half Femtosecond laser systems combine femtosecond lasers with other traditional surgical tools, such as manual incision instruments. These systems offer surgeons flexibility by allowing them to choose between femtosecond and traditional methods for different steps in a surgery.</p></p>
