<p><p>Solid State Transformers (SST) are advanced power electronic devices that can operate at high frequency and voltage levels. They serve as an alternative to traditional transformers and offer various benefits such as increased efficiency, compact size, and improved reliability. The SST market is classified into three types: </p><p>1. Two-stage: These SSTs involve two conversion stages, often including both AC to DC and DC to AC conversion, allowing for enhanced flexibility and control.</p><p>3. Three-stage: This market segment includes SSTs that incorporate three stages of conversion, providing even more precise power conversion capabilities and enabling advanced functionality.</p></p>
