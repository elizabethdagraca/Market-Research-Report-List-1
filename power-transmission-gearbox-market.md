<p><p>The power transmission gearbox market comprises various types of gearboxes, including helical, planetary, bevel, and others. Helical gearboxes utilize parallel or inclined teeth to transfer power more smoothly and quietly. Planetary gearboxes use multiple smaller gears orbiting a central gear, offering higher torque and reduction ratio capabilities. Bevel gearboxes transmit power between intersecting shafts at specific angles, often used in automotive and industrial applications. "Others" include gearboxes with unique designs or a combination of different gear types, catering to specific power transmission requirements in different industries.</p></p>
