<p><p>Hydro turbines are the devices used to convert the kinetic energy of fast-flowing or falling water into mechanical energy for generating electricity. There are different types of hydro turbines in the market. Francis turbines are commonly used in large-scale projects with medium to high head and flow rates. Kaplan turbines are suitable for low head applications and offer adjustable blades. Pelton turbines are perfect for high head and lower flow rates. Finally, the "Others" market consists of various turbines, such as cross-flow and Gorlov helical turbines, catering to specific project requirements and conditions.</p></p>
