<p><p>The portable alcohol detector market is categorized into three main types: fuel cell alcohol sensor, semiconductor alcohol sensor, and others. Other types may include infrared-based or spectrophotometry-based technologies. Each type has its own advantages and limitations, such as accuracy, sensitivity, cost, and power consumption. These detectors are used to ensure safety in various sectors, including law enforcement, healthcare, and personal use.</p></p>
