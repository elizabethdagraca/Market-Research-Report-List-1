<p><ul><li>Bottled Water Cooler (BWC)</li><li>Point-of-Use (POU)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water coolers and dispensers market can be classified into two main types: Bottled Water Cooler (BWC) and Point-of-Use (POU). BWCs are freestanding units that require bottles of water to be replaced periodically. They are suitable for places with limited access to a water supply. On the other hand, POU dispensers are connected directly to the water source, eliminating the need for bottle replacements. They are ideal for locations with a reliable water supply and are more cost-effective in the long run.</p></p>
