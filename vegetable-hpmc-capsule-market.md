<p><p>The HPMC (Hydroxypropyl Methylcellulose) vegetable capsule market can be divided into two types: HPMC with Gelling Agent and HPMC without Gelling Agent. HPMC with Gelling Agent capsules are formulated with additional ingredients that enhance the gelling properties of the capsule shell, allowing it to form a gel-like substance when in contact with fluids. On the other hand, HPMC without Gelling Agent capsules do not contain any additional gelling agents, resulting in a more transparent and non-gel forming capsule shell. These variations cater to different requirements in the pharmaceutical and nutraceutical industries.</p></p>
