<p><p>Search Engine Marketing (SEM) Tools Market includes two primary types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise SEM tools are installed directly onto the user's system, offering full control and customization but requiring maintenance and updates. On the other hand, Cloud-Based SEM tools are hosted on remote servers accessible via the internet. They provide convenience, scalability, and automatic updates but may have limited customization options.
