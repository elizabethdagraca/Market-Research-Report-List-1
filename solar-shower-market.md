<p><p>The solar shower market can be categorized into two main types: Fixed and Portable. Fixed solar showers are installed permanently in a specific location, typically outdoors, and use solar panels to heat up water for showers. They are commonly found in homes, hotels, or recreational facilities. On the other hand, portable solar showers are designed to be easily carried and used anywhere. They are compact, lightweight, and often come with their own water reservoirs or bags. Portable solar showers are popular among campers, hikers, and outdoor enthusiasts who need a convenient bathing solution while on the move.</p></p>
