<p><p>The Bluetooth audio SoC market can be categorized into two types: the ordinary Bluetooth chip and the smart Bluetooth chip. An ordinary Bluetooth chip refers to a basic chip that provides wireless audio connectivity. It handles basic audio functions and is commonly used in simple Bluetooth devices like headphones and speakers. On the other hand, a smart Bluetooth chip offers advanced features beyond audio connectivity, such as integration with voice assistants, gesture control, and health monitoring. These chips are typically found in more sophisticated audio devices like smart speakers and wearable gadgets.</p></p>
