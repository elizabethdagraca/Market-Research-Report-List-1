<p><p>The networking cables market is comprised of various types, including coaxial cables, twisted pair cables, and optical fiber cables. Coaxial cables are widely used for transmitting high-frequency signals and are commonly used in cable television and internet connections. Twisted pair cables consist of pairs of insulated wires twisted together, offering reliable data transmission and are commonly used in Ethernet networks. Optical fiber cables use thin strands of glass or plastic to transmit data through light signals, providing high-speed and long-distance communication, often used in telecommunications and internet infrastructure.</p></p>
