<p><p>Effluent pumps are used to discharge wastewater or sewage from septic tanks or sewage basins. The PW type of effluent pump is designed for use in residential or light commercial applications. These pumps are capable of handling relatively clean liquid with suspended solids. On the other hand, the PWL type is a more robust pump commonly used in commercial and industrial settings. It is specifically designed to handle more challenging wastewater, including liquids with larger solids or higher concentrations of chemicals. Both types play a crucial role in efficiently removing wastewater from various sources.</p></p>
