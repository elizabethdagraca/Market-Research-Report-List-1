<p><p>The agricultural pump market consists of various types of pumps that serve different purposes. Submersible pumps are designed to be submerged in water, commonly used for deep wells or irrigation systems. Self-priming pumps are able to evacuate air from the suction line and operate on their own, typically used for dewatering applications. Vortex pumps have an impeller that creates a whirlpool effect, suitable for transferring dirty or abrasive liquids. Lastly, there are other specialized pumps available for specific agricultural needs, such as slurry pumps or chemical pumps.</p></p>
