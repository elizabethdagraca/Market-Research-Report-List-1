<p><p>An infusion extension tube is a medical device used to deliver fluids or medication from an infusion pump to the patient's body. It typically consists of a plastic tube with connectors at both ends, one of which attaches to the infusion pump and the other to the patient's catheter or needle. These tubes are used in a variety of healthcare settings, including hospitals, clinics, and home healthcare.</p><p>The infusion extension tube market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, such as cancer and diabetes, is one of the key factors driving market growth. These advancements enhance the accuracy and safety of infusion therapy, leading to increased adoption of infusion extension tubes.</p><p>There is also a growing trend of using disposable infusion extension tubes to minimize the risk of contamination and infection.
