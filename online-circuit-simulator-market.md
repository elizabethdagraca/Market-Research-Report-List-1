<p><strong>The Online Circuit Simulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cloud-based</li><li>On-premises</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The online circuit simulator market can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based circuit simulators are hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. They offer flexibility, scalability, and ease of access from anywhere, making them suitable for users who prefer convenience and low infrastructure costs. On the other hand, on-premises circuit simulators are installed locally on the user's hardware, providing higher control, security, and customization options. These are favored by users who prioritize data privacy, have specific requirements, or need offline access to the simulator.</p></p>
