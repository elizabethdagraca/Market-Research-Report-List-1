<p><ul><li>Magnetic Bar</li><li>Optical Guidance (OG)</li><li>Magnetic Tape</li><li>Laster Guidance</li><li>Indoor Global Positioning System</li><li>Attitude Heading Reference System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are various market types for path guidance systems. Magnetic bar systems use magnets embedded in the ground to guide vehicles. Optical guidance involves cameras and sensors to follow painted lines on the floor. Magnetic tape systems use tape strips to guide vehicles. Laser guidance relies on laser beams and detectors to navigate. Indoor Global Positioning System utilizes satellite technology to track vehicle positions. Attitude Heading Reference System incorporates sensors to determine vehicle orientation. These market types offer different solutions for guiding vehicles along specific paths with precision and accuracy.</p></p>
