<p><p>IT Operations and Service Management (ITOSM) combines the disciplines of IT Service Management (ITSM) and IT Operations Management (ITOM). ITSM focuses on managing IT services and delivering value to customers, while ITOM focuses on managing the technical side of IT operations. The ITSM market involves the implementation of processes and tools to improve service delivery, incident management, problem management, and change management. On the other hand, the ITOM market involves managing the infrastructure, devices, and networks that support IT services, including tasks like server monitoring, network performance management, and automation.</p></p>
