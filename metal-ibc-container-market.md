Manufacturers are equipping these containers with features such as pressure relief valves, overflow control systems, and RFID tracking capabilities to ensure proper handling and monitoring of the stored products.</p><p>Another emerging trend is the growing demand for customized IBC containers to meet specific requirements of different industries.
