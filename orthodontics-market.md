<p><p>Orthodontics market types include brackets, anchorage appliances, and archwires. Brackets are devices used to hold the orthodontic archwire in place on the teeth, allowing for controlled movement. Anchorage appliances are used to provide stability and prevent unwanted tooth movement during orthodontic treatment. Archwires are the main component of braces, serving as a guide for tooth movement. They are inserted into the brackets and exert pressure to align the teeth properly. These three types of orthodontic products play crucial roles in correcting dental misalignments and achieving desired orthodontic results.</p></p>
