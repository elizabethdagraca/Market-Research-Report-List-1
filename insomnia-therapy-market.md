<p><p>Insomnia therapy market encompasses two main types: non-pharmacological therapy and pharmacological therapy. Non-pharmacological therapy focuses on treating insomnia without the use of drugs, employing techniques such as cognitive-behavioral therapy, relaxation therapy, and sleep hygiene practices. This approach aims to address underlying psychological and behavioral factors contributing to sleep disturbances. In contrast, pharmacological therapy involves the use of medications to manage insomnia symptoms. These medications may include hypnotics, sedatives, or antihistamines, which aim to induce sleep or promote sleep duration. Both approaches offer different strategies for individuals seeking treatment for their insomnia.</p></p>
