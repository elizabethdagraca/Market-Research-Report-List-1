<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Digital ICs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microprocessors</li><li>Microcontrollers</li><li>DSPs</li><li>Logic Devices</li><li>Memory</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital ICs, or Integrated Circuits, are electronic circuits that contain thousands or millions of transistors on a small silicon chip. They are categorized into different types based on their functions and applications. </p><p>Microprocessors are digital ICs that act as the brain of a computer, controlling and executing instructions. </p><p>Microcontrollers are similar to microprocessors but have built-in memory and peripherals, making them suitable for embedded systems and IoT devices. </p><p>DSPs, or Digital Signal Processors, are specialized ICs that process digital signals, commonly used in audio and video processing applications. </p><p>Logic Devices are ICs that perform logic functions such as AND, OR, and NOT, enabling the creation of complex digital circuits. </p><p>Memory market refers to ICs that store and retrieve data, such as Random Access Memory (RAM) and Read-Only Memory (ROM).</p></p>
