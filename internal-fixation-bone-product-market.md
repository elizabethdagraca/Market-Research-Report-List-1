<p><p>Internal fixation bone products are medical devices used to stabilize fractures or bone deformities during the healing process. The market for these products includes different types such as plates, screws, and nails. Plates are metal plates that are attached to the surface of the bone using screws, providing stability and support. Screws are metallic or non-metallic devices that are inserted into the bone to hold the fractured parts together. Nails, also known as intramedullary rods, are inserted into the medullary canal of long bones for stability and alignment. These products play a crucial role in aiding the recovery and rehabilitation of patients with bone fractures or deformities.</p></p>
