<p><strong>The Optical Turbidimeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scattered Light Turbidimeter</li><li>Transmitted Light Turbidimeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Turbidimeter market includes two main types: Scattered Light Turbidimeters and Transmitted Light Turbidimeters. Scattered Light Turbidimeters measure the intensity of light scattered by suspended particles in a liquid sample, providing an indirect measure of turbidity. They are widely used in industries such as wastewater treatment and environmental monitoring. On the other hand, Transmitted Light Turbidimeters measure the reduction in light transmitted through a liquid sample due to the presence of suspended particles, providing a direct measure of turbidity. These are commonly used in industries like pharmaceuticals and beverage production.</p></p>
