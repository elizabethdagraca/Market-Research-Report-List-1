<p><p>Oil centrifuges are used for separating oil-water mixtures in various industries. They come in three main types: swinging-bucket, fixed-angle, and vertical rotors. Swinging-bucket rotors are designed to hold multiple sample tubes and swing outwards during operation, ensuring efficient separation. Fixed-angle rotors hold the sample tubes at a fixed angle, allowing for higher speeds and improved pellet formation. Vertical rotors are ideal for larger sample volumes and provide efficient separation due to their vertical orientation. These different types cater to diverse requirements in the oil centrifuges market.</p></p>
