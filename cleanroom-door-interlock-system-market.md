<p><p>Cleanroom door interlock systems are designed to maintain a controlled environment within cleanrooms or containment areas. The market offers different types of interlock systems, including double-door interlock, three-door interlock, four-door interlock, and others. Double-door interlock systems ensure that only one door is open at a time, preventing cross-contamination. Three-door interlock systems allow one door to open only when the adjacent door is closed, ensuring controlled access. Four-door interlock systems operate similarly, but with an added door for increased security. The "others" category may include customized interlock systems based on specific requirements. These systems play a critical role in maintaining hygiene and safety within cleanrooms.</p></p>
