<p><p>The Non-Destructive Digging (NDD) service market consists of two main types: hydraulic excavation and hydraulic and dry suction excavation. Hydraulic excavation involves using pressurized water streams to break soil or other materials, which are then vacuumed away. This method is commonly used for soft or sandy soil. On the other hand, hydraulic and dry suction excavation uses a combination of water and air vacuum to remove materials, making it suitable for both wet and dry soils. These methods are employed for safe and precise digging without damaging underground utilities or structures.</p></p>
