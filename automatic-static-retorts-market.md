<p><p>Automatic static retorts are used in food processing to safely and efficiently sterilize packaged products. There are various types of automatic static retorts available in the market. Steam and water spray retort uses steam and water spray to heat and sterilize the products. Water immersion retort fully immerses the packages in hot water. Air steam retort uses a combination of hot air and steam to sterilize the products. These different types of retorts offer diverse benefits and suit different food processing requirements.</p></p>
