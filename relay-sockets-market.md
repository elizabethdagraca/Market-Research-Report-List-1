<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Relay Sockets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DIN Rail</li><li>Panel Mount</li><li>Surface Mount</li><li>PCB Mount</li><li>Other Mount</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Relay sockets refer to devices used to hold and connect relays to a circuit board. There are several types of relay socket markets available. DIN rail sockets are designed to be mounted on a standard DIN rail. Panel mount sockets are installed on the front panel of an electrical enclosure. Surface mount sockets are directly soldered onto the circuit board. PCB mount sockets are soldered onto the circuit board as well but are typically used for through-hole mounting. Other mount sockets include options like socket track mounts, which provide flexibility in mounting choices.</p></p>
