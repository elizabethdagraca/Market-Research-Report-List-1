<p><strong>The Heart Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Implantable Heart Pump Devices</li><li>Extracorporeal Heart Pump Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heart pump market can be categorized into two types: implantable heart pump devices and extracorporeal heart pump devices. Implantable heart pump devices are surgically implanted inside the patient's body to help the heart pump blood effectively. They are typically used in cases of severe heart failure. On the other hand, extracorporeal heart pump devices are external devices that support the heart's pumping function by temporarily taking over its responsibilities. These devices are used during open-heart surgeries or for patients awaiting heart transplantation.</p></p>
