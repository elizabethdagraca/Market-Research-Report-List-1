<p><p>The HVAC valve market consists of various types such as ball valve, globe valve, butterfly valve, and others. A ball valve is a quarter-turn valve that uses a hollow, perforated ball to control the flow of fluid. A globe valve controls flow by raising or lowering a movable disk into or out of the flow path. Butterfly valves use a rotating disc to regulate the fluid flow. The "others" category includes different types like gate valves, check valves, and plug valves, each serving specific purposes in the HVAC industry.</p></p>
