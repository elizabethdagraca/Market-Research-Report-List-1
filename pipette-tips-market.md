<p><strong>The Pipette Tips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Filtered Pipette Tips</li><li>Filtered Pipette Tips</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pipette tips are essential laboratory accessories used to transfer and dispense precise volumes of liquids. Non-filtered pipette tips refer to those without any additional particle or aerosol protection, commonly used for basic liquid handling tasks. On the other hand, filtered pipette tips have built-in filters that prevent contamination and the introduction of aerosols or microbes into samples, making them ideal for sensitive applications like PCR or molecular biology. Both types cater to different laboratory needs in terms of sterility and protection.</p></p>
