<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The CCTV Telephoto Zoom Lens market is divided into two main types: CS Mount and C Mount. </p><p>CS Mount lenses have a shorter flange back distance, which allows them to be mounted closer to the image sensor, resulting in improved image quality and higher resolution. They are commonly used in modern surveillance cameras.</p><p>On the other hand, C Mount lenses have a longer flange back distance and are compatible with older cameras. They offer flexibility due to their detachable lens capability, making them suitable for various applications and camera types. However, their image quality and resolution may not match that of CS Mount lenses.</p></p>
