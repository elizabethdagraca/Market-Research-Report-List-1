<p><p>Tempering furnaces are used for heat treating metals to increase their strength and durability. There are two types of tempering furnaces available in the market. Batch type furnaces allow for the simultaneous tempering of multiple products in a single cycle, making them suitable for small to medium-sized operations. On the other hand, continuous type furnaces offer a continuous tempering process, making them ideal for large-scale production. Both types of furnaces play a crucial role in various industries, such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, by ensuring the desired mechanical properties of metal components.</p></p>
