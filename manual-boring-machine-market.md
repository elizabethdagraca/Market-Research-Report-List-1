<p><p>The Manual Boring Machine Market can be categorized into two types: Vertical Boring Machine and Horizontal Boring Machine. A Vertical Boring Machine is used for drilling or enlarging existing holes vertically. It is commonly used for tasks such as drilling water wells or drilling shafts for mining purposes. On the other hand, a Horizontal Boring Machine is used for drilling horizontally, typically used in construction or pipeline installation.
