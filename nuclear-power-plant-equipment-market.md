<p><p>The nuclear power plant equipment market can be divided into two main types: nuclear island equipment and auxiliary systems. Nuclear island equipment refers to the essential components involved in the process of nuclear power generation, including the reactor, turbine, and steam generator. These components are responsible for producing the necessary heat and converting it into electricity. On the other hand, auxiliary systems support the operation of nuclear island equipment and include components such as cooling systems, pumps, valves, and control systems. Together, these equipment and systems ensure the safe and efficient functioning of nuclear power plants.</p></p>
