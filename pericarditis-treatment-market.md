<p><p>Pericarditis is a medical condition characterized by the inflammation of the pericardium, which is the sac-like membrane surrounding the heart. If the condition is caused by a bacterial infection, antibiotics are generally administered.</p><p>Corticosteroids may be prescribed in certain cases to reduce inflammation, especially if the symptoms persist despite NSAID treatment.
