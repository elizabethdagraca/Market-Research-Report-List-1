<p><strong>The Commercial Inkjet Printhead Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Type</li><li>Thermal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial inkjet printhead market consists of two main types: Piezoelectric type and Thermal type. Piezoelectric printheads use electric currents to create pressure waves that eject ink droplets from nozzles. Thermal printheads, on the other hand, use heat to quickly vaporize ink and create bubbles that propel droplets onto the paper. Both types offer unique advantages, with Piezoelectric printheads known for their durability and precision, while Thermal printheads are valued for their speed and cost-effectiveness in high-volume printing applications.</p></p>
