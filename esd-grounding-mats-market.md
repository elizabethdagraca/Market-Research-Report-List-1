Desk mats are smaller mats placed on workstations or desks, providing a static-dissipative surface for grounding. They are convenient for preventing electrostatic discharge while working on electronics. On the other hand, floor mats are larger mats placed on the floor, allowing workers to stand on them while working. These mats provide a conductive path for static electricity to ground, reducing the risk of ESD-related damage and hazards in larger working environments.</p></p>
