<p><p>720P projectors are available in different market types such as LCD, DLP, LCOS, and others. LCD projectors use liquid crystal displays to produce images, offering bright and vibrant colors. DLP projectors use digital micromirror devices to create images with high contrast and sharpness. LCOS projectors utilize liquid crystal on silicon technology, combining the advantages of LCD and DLP projectors to deliver exceptional image quality. "Others" refer to alternative technologies like LBS (laser beam scanning) or LCoQ (liquid-crystal on quartz) that offer unique features or advancements in the display of 720P projectors.</p></p>
