<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Diffuse Mode Sensor market comprises various types such as Diffused with Background Suppression, Diffused with Foreground Suppression, Diffused with Background Evaluation, and Others. Diffused with Background Suppression sensors use a light beam that is diffusely reflected by an object, while suppressing signals from objects beyond the background. Diffused with Foreground Suppression sensors differentiate between objects close to the sensor and objects in the background, inhibiting background signals. Diffused with Background Evaluation sensors detect objects by evaluating the reflection intensity from the background. Lastly, the "Others" category includes different types of diffused mode sensors not specifically mentioned above.</p></p>
