<p><p>The Optical Mark Recognition (OMR) Scanner market is divided into two types: Single Side Reading Type and Dual Side Reading Type. Single Side Reading Type scanners read and process marks on one side of the paper, making them suitable for simple applications such as surveys or exams with limited data. On the other hand, Dual Side Reading Type scanners can process marks on both sides of the paper simultaneously, providing increased efficiency and speed for complex tasks that require more data processing, like large-scale exams or election ballots.</p></p>
