<p><p>Optical Thickness Measurement Systems are used to measure the thickness of various materials. The Contact market refers to systems that require physical contact between the measuring device and the material being measured. These systems typically use sensors or probes to directly measure the thickness. On the other hand, the Non-Contact market refers to systems that can measure thickness without any physical contact with the material. These systems use advanced technologies like lasers or ultrasound waves to measure the thickness accurately without touching the material.</p></p>
