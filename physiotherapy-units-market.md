Electrotherapy units utilize electrical currents to alleviate pain and stimulate muscles. Magnetotherapy units employ magnetic fields to improve blood flow and reduce inflammation. These different types of physiotherapy units offer diverse treatment options for patients with a variety of conditions.</p></p>
