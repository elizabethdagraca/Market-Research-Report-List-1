Copying milling machines: These machines are used to replicate a specific part or design by following a template or pattern.</p><p>2. Program control milling machines: These machines are operated by pre-programmed commands, allowing for precise and repetitive milling operations.</p><p>3. CNC milling machines: These machines are controlled by computer numerical control, offering high levels of automation, accuracy, and versatility in milling operations. Each type of machine offers unique capabilities and advantages for various milling applications.</p></p>
